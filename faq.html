How can I adopt a pet?

To adopt a pet, you can visit our shelter during our adoption hours and speak with one of our adoption counselors. They will guide you through the adoption process and help you find the perfect pet for your home.

What are the requirements for adopting a pet?

The requirements for adopting a pet may vary depending on the animal shelter's policies. Generally, you will need to provide identification, proof of residence, and demonstrate that you are capable of providing a loving and responsible home for the pet.

Can I volunteer at AdoptMe?

Yes, we welcome volunteers! You can fill out a volunteer application form on our website or visit our shelter to inquire about volunteer opportunities. Volunteers play a crucial role in helping us care for our animals and support our organization.
